#### `bounds(input: a series of number | any[] | Mat | Tensor | number, dim : 0 or 1 or 2)`

**param** `input` a series of number, or an array, matrix, tensor to get the min and max

**param** `dim` (optional and defualt 0) - 0 (bound of all), 1 (bound of column), 2(bound of row)

**returns: number** - 1-d array or 2-d matrix [min, max], [[min1, min2], [max1, max2]], [[min1, max1], [min2, max2]]

This function simply finds the minimuim and maximum value of input. 
You can use dim to determine that you get the bounds of all values or according to row or column.
