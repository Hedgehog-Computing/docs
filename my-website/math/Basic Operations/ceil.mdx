#### `ceil(input: a series of number | any[] | Mat | Tensor | number, dim : 0 or 1 or 2)`

**param** `input` a number, or an array, matrix, tensor to get the ceiling. 

**returns** - number, or JS array, or matrix, or tensor

This function simply finds the ceiling of numbers. For example ceil(1.5) = 2, ceil(-1.5) = -1
