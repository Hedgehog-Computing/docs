#### `iqr(input: a JS array, matrix or tensor)`

**param** `input` a JS array, matrix or tensor, or a series of numbers

**returns** - a number representing iqr

Iqr means: firstly we find three borderline points q1, q2, q3 with quantile 0.25, 0.5, 0.75. Then we return q3 - q1
